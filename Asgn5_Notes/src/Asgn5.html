  • So welcome to the Miror B vs Serena 6v6 Pokemon Battle!

Instructions for Playing:
  • Each turn the player on the right (Serena) will make their attack choice first.
  • To attack, click the your Pokemon in the center and choose an attack.
  • You may also view that Pokemon's stats or any other Pokemon on your team by clicking on them.
  • Player 2 will then make their choice and the turn will play out.
  • When a Pokemon faints, that player should select another Pokemon from their bench.
  • Fight until 1 player wins as the MASTER POKEMON TRAINER!
Future Features (if I choose to build onto it):
  • Allow Pokemon Switching
  • Add abilities and effects like poison, burn, freeze
  • Long Term: Refactor to use PokeAPI and allow teams to be chosen and generated
