  • This project contains 29 Primary Shapes/Objects:
    • 12 Pokeballs
    • 12 Pokemon .obj Models
    • 2 Pokemon Trainer .obj Models
    • 1 Multi-Texture Cube
    • 2 Spinning Cylinders
    • 1 Floor Plane

  • This project contains 3 Lights:
    • Directional Light: White, pointing from the northwest at a southeast angle
    • Hemisphere Light: Aqua Sky and Tan Ground, to tint the top of the models and mimic the ground texture
    • Spotlight Light: White, to highlight the battle on the arena!
  • A skybox in the form of an HDR Pokemon Center.
  • Orbit Controls for the camera.
